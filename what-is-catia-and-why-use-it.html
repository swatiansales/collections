What is CATIA and why use it?

CATIA is most popular, innovating and widely used CAD(computer aided design) tool which stands for "Computer Aided Three-Dimensional Interactive Application". In simple words, it is product design software.

CATIA first version was CATIA V1 which has been released in 1981 by Dassault Systems. The latest verion of CATIA is known as CATIA V6.

CATIA is a form of multi platform software suite which includes three advance tools like - CAD, CAE (Computer-Aided Engineering) and CAM (Computer-Aided Manufacture).

What is CAD, CAE and CAM?
CAD is used for creating the 2D drawings and 3D models of real products, digitally before manufacturing.

CAE is used to describe the procedure of he whole product engineering process from design to virtual testing and check by using analytical logic.

CAM is used for computer-controlled machinery to automate a manufacturing process. You can collect the interview questions at “CATIA interview questions and answers” if you preparing a career in CATIA.

CATIA has numerous functionalities which are available in the form of workbenches or piece of software. The workbenches availability depends on your purchased license. You do not need to purchase the whole software which is very costly.

CATIA is a powerful and innovative application but has complex learning curve so it may take some time to become master of CATIA. It has built-in modification management abilities which is very helpful in automatic updates.

CATIA is mostly used for Computer Aided Design(CAD) . CATIA have six modules in CAD platform -

  • CATIA’s sketching - A fully defined sketches can be sketched easily in CATIA.
  • CATIA’s Part Modelling - You can design almost anything in CATIA. It is mostly used for solid modelling.
  • CATIA’s Surfacing - It is used for body designing in automotive and aerospace insustry.
  • CATIA’s Sheetmetal - It deals in sheetmetal module that formed into thin sheets by rolling or hammering.
  • CATIA’s Assembly - You can produce simulations of products using assemly mechanism in CATIA.
  • CATIA’s Drafting - It is used for drawing that communicate to desinger, collaborators, product departemnt, etc.

CATIA is used widely by a variety of industries e. g. -  automotive, aerospace, defense and industrial equipment, plant design, consumer packaged goods, architecture and construction, process power and petroleum and other services.

CATIA is used in the Aerospace industry for example - the development of the Indian Light Combat Aircraft
